Scope & General Conditions
Our Price Includes:
Thorough protection of floors, cabinets, railings
Removal and reinstallation of any wall plates or wall hardware as needed
Repairs of minor surface imperfections and caulking of trim gaps, as required (~1h per space)
Primer on bare substrates, and 2 coats of high-end paint on all designated surfaces
Thorough preparation on all wooden trim incl. cleaning + priming with ultra adherent primer
Thorough preparation on leaded glass windows incl. scraping of loose paint & mastic, resurfacing, priming, and caulking
Final clean up at the end of the work
General Conditions
The space is expected to be vacant and free of furniture during the work
Our quote excludes repairs — surfaces are presumed to have been plastered, sanded, and cleaned beforehand
Previously painted surfaces are presumed to be latex-based; oil-based surfaces would require an additional priming coat
Any work outside of the scope of this quote will be billed at $65/h + materials, as demanded
