### Line Item Description Conventions

- **Preparation is almost never a separate line item** — it is bundled into each section total, except in a few quotes (LARUE, DEUTSCHMAN, BRADY) where "Preparation" and "Painting" are split as two sub-items within a section.
- **Scaffolding is always broken into two lines**: rental cost + installation/dismantling cost. These are occasionally nested within a façade section rather than a standalone section.
- **Optional add-ons are clearly flagged**: `[OPTION]`, `[OPTION EN EXTRA]`, `[Optional Add-Ons]` — and are consistently excluded from the subtotal/grand total.
- **Repairs are almost always excluded** from the fixed quote price and noted as estimated ranges (e.g., `[1000 - 2000$]`) charged at hourly rate.
- **Some quotes itemize by preparation step** (scraping, priming, painting 2 coats as separate lines — e.g., LARUE front balcony: $1,200 scraping + $900 priming + $1,100 painting).
- **Later quotes (2025)** more frequently use `[approx. X$ - Y$]` inline notes beside line items, and include explicit disclaimers that the quote is an **estimate, not a fixed price**.

## 3. Paint Product Patterns

### Primary Exterior Finish Coats

| Product | Frequency | Typical Use | Finish |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sherwin Williams Duration Exterior** | Dominant (~15 quotes) | All exterior surfaces — stucco, wood, fascia, soffits, trim | Satin (most common); Eggshell mentioned once; finish often unspecified |
| **Sherwin Williams LOXON** | Medium (~3 quotes) | Stucco / masonry primer or direct coat | Not specified (primer-grade) |
| **Sherwin Williams Latitude** | Low (~2 quotes) | Exterior paint on fascias, doors, balconies | Not specified |
| **Sico ProLuxe (formerly Sikkens)** | Medium (~3 quotes) | Wood stain — beams, siding, balcony elements | Semi-transparent to solid stain |
| **Sansin SDF** | Low (~2 quotes) | Natural wood stain — fascias, soffits, handrails | Matte (Mat) |
| **Benjamin Moore Aura Exterior** | Rare (~1 quote) | General exterior (Legault) | Not specified |
| **BEHR Ultra** | Rare (~1 quote) | Windows, garage doors (Boretsky) | Semi-gloss |
| **Storm Stain — Enduradeck (Opaque)** | Rare (~1 quote) | Balcony floor / steps (Dumont) | Opaque |
| **Behr Opaque Stain** | Rare (~1 quote) | General (Legault) | Opaque |
| **Sansin DEC** | Rare (~1 quote) | Wood exterior (Legault early version) | Not specified |
| **SW ArmorSeal Tread-Plex** | Rare (~1 quote) | Front steps (Cobbler) | Not specified |

### Primers

| Product | Use |
|---|---|
| **SW Exterior Oil-Based Wood Primer** | Bare or bare-ish wood surfaces — standard primer before Duration Ext |
| **Sherwin Williams LOXON (masonry primer)** | Stucco and masonry primer |
| **Kembond Metal Oil Primer** | Metal surfaces, anti-rust applications |
| **ProCry Industrial Grade Galvanized Metal Primer** | Metal (Campbell, 2025) |
| **Apprêt à l'huile (generic)** | Wood primer when product not specified |

### Key Observations
- **Sherwin Williams Duration Exterior** is the clear house standard for all painted wood and mixed surfaces.
- **Satin** is the most commonly specified finish when finish is stated.
- **Stucco surfaces** get LOXON as primer; Duration Exterior as finish coat.
- **Wood staining** uses Sico Pro
