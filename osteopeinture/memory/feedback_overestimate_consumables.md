---
name: Overestimate consumables per job
description: When estimating per-job supplies/consumables, err on the side of overestimating rather than underestimating
type: feedback
---

Per-job consumable allocations have historically been underestimated by ~$16-19K/year (2025 data). Loric's directive: overestimate rather than underestimate.

**Why:** Underestimating inflates per-job profitability, which leads to owners thinking they earned more than they did. Better to show conservative margins and be pleasantly surprised.

**How to apply:** When building any tool that estimates or allocates consumables to jobs, use higher estimates. When presenting per-job P&L, note that consumable allocations are estimates and actual costs may be lower.
