| Equipment | Daily | Weekly | Monthly |

## Delivery
- $165 each way
- Standard 2 trips per job (deliver + pickup) = **$330**

## Harness
- $20/day, $60/week, $150/month
- Comes with sling/lanyard included

## Notes
- GAMMA is the primary lift supplier for OstéoPeinture (EMCO is primary for scaffold)
- Z34 and Z45 are the most commonly used
- Compare EMCO lift prices before ordering — EMCO has scissor lifts and personnel lifts at lower rates for lower heights
